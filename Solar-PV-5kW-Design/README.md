
##  5kW Residential Solar PV System Design (Off-Grid Case Study)

###  Objective
Design a **5kW off-grid residential solar PV system** to supply the daily energy demand of a household, ensuring energy independence and reliable power supply.

---

## Project Overview

This project focuses on the design and basic sizing of a standalone solar PV system. It includes system components selection, load estimation, and conceptual layout design.

### Key Design Considerations
- Daily energy demand estimation
- Solar panel sizing (5kW system capacity)
- Battery storage for off-grid operation
- Charge controller selection
- System efficiency and losses
- Safety and reliability factors

---

## Tools Used
- AutoCAD (for layout/design drawings)
- Excel (for load and system calculations)
- Manual engineering calculations

## System Components (Summary)
- Solar PV Panels (5kW total capacity)
- Battery Bank (for energy storage)
- Inverter (DC to AC conversion)
- Charge Controller (MPPT/PWM)
- Electrical wiring and protection devices

---

## Outcome
- Designed a fully off-grid capable residential solar PV system
- Ensured continuous energy supply based on estimated load
- Optimized system sizing for efficiency and reliability
